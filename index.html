SproutZen (“SproutZen”, “the App”, Chinese name: 花小绿) is a garden journal and care-reminder app with optional AI features. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the App and how we handle it.

Developer contact: dylanappfly@163.com

1. Summary

  • Most of your data (plants, care logs, reminders, settings) stays on your device.
  • We do not require an account and do not sell your personal information.
  • AI features send photos and related context to third-party AI services through secure proxies to generate results.
  • Subscriptions are processed by the Apple App Store via RevenueCat; we do not receive your payment card details.

2. Information stored on your device

The App uses local storage (SQLite) on your phone or tablet for:

  • Plants in your garden, nicknames, photos you add, and care history
  • Care schedules, reminder settings, and notification preferences
  • AI usage credits / subscription state cached locally for offline display
  • Language preference and optional home-screen plant image
  • Consent choices (e.g. location for AI diagnosis, saving images to the gallery)

This data is not automatically uploaded to servers operated by us.

3. Permissions and how we use them

Camera & photo library

Used when you choose to take or select photos for:

  • AI plant identification
  • AI health diagnosis
  • AI Studio (stylized plant artwork)
  • Plant gallery and home-screen display images
  • Saving or sharing generated images

Location (when in use)

Requested only for AI health diagnosis if you agree. The App uses approximate location to infer city/region, climate zone, and recent weather (via a public weather API) so care advice can reflect local conditions. You can decline; diagnosis still works without climate context.

Notifications

Used for local care reminders (watering, fertilizing, etc.) and optional notifications when AI Studio artwork finishes generating. Reminder content is scheduled on your device; we do not operate a push-notification server for care data.

4. Information sent over the network

AI features

When you use AI identification, diagnosis, or Studio artwork, the App may send:

  • Photos you selected (typically as compressed JPEG)
  • Short text context (plant name, care schedule summary, climate summary, locale)
  • A short-lived authentication token (JWT) to our Cloudflare Worker proxies

Our proxies forward requests to AI model providers such as Alibaba Cloud DashScope (Qwen) and/or Cloudflare Workers AI, depending on server configuration. Those providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies. We do not use your photos to train our own models.

Weather data

For climate context, the App may call Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to obtain recent local weather summaries. No account is required.

5. Purchases and subscriptions

Optional in-app purchases (subscriptions and AI credit packs) are handled by Apple through RevenueCat. We receive subscription status and product identifiers to unlock features; we do not receive your full payment card number. See Apple’s privacy policy for payment processing.

6. What we do not collect

  • No mandatory user account or email registration in the App
  • No advertising SDKs or cross-app tracking for ads
  • No sale of personal information

7. Children

The App is a general plant-care tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data retention and deletion

  • On device: Delete plants in the App or uninstall the App to remove local data.
  • AI providers: Retention is governed by the relevant AI service provider.
  • Purchases: Transaction records are retained by Apple as required by its policies.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date and, where appropriate, in the App.

10. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: dylanappfly@163.com

一、概要

  • 您的大部分数据（植物、养护记录、提醒、设置）默认保存在本机。
  • 本应用不要求注册账号，我们不出售您的个人信息。
  • 使用 AI 功能时，您选择的照片及相关上下文会通过安全代理发送至第三方 AI 服务以生成结果。
  • 订阅与内购由 App Store 经 RevenueCat 处理，我们不会收到您的银行卡完整信息。

二、保存在您设备上的信息

本应用使用设备本地数据库（SQLite）存储：

  • 花园中的植物、昵称、您添加的照片与养护记录
  • 养护计划、提醒设置与通知偏好
  • AI 额度 / 订阅状态的本地缓存（便于离线展示）
  • 语言偏好与可选的首页展示图
  • 您的授权选择（如 AI 诊断是否使用位置、是否保存图片到相册）

上述数据不会自动上传至我们运营的服务器。

三、权限及用途

相机与相册

仅在您主动拍照或选图时使用，用于：

  • AI 植物识别
  • AI 健康诊断
  • AI 画坊（风格化植物形象）
  • 植物相册与首页展示图
  • 保存或分享生成的图片

位置（使用期间）

仅在您同意时，用于 AI 健康诊断：根据大致位置推断城市/地区、气候带， 并通过公开天气接口获取近期天气，使养护建议更贴合当地环境。 您可拒绝授权；拒绝后仍可诊断，只是不包含气候上下文。

通知

用于本地养护提醒（浇水、施肥等）及画坊生图完成时的可选提醒。 提醒内容在本机调度，我们不针对养护数据运营远程推送服务器。

四、通过网络传输的信息

AI 功能

使用 AI 识别、诊断或画坊时，可能发送：

  • 您选择的照片（通常为压缩后的 JPEG）
  • 简短文本上下文（植物名称、养护摘要、气候摘要、界面语言等）
  • 短期有效的鉴权令牌（JWT），用于访问我们的 Cloudflare Worker 代理

代理将请求转发至 AI 模型服务商，例如阿里云百炼（通义/Qwen） 和/或 Cloudflare Workers AI（取决于服务端配置）。 相关处理受各服务商条款与隐私政策约束。我们不会将您的照片用于训练自有模型。

天气数据

气候上下文可能调用 Open-Meteo （open-meteo.com） 获取近期当地天气摘要，无需注册账号。

可选的内购（订阅与 AI 次数包）由 Apple 通过 RevenueCat 处理。我们仅接收订阅状态与商品标识以解锁功能。

  • 不强制注册账号或收集邮箱
  • 不使用广告 SDK 或跨应用广告追踪
  • 不出售个人信息

七、儿童

本应用为面向大众的植物养护工具，并非专门针对 13 岁以下儿童。 我们不会故意收集儿童的个人信息。

八、数据保留与删除

  • 本机数据：在应用内删除植物，或卸载应用即可清除本地数据。
  • AI 服务商：保留期限以对应 AI 服务商政策为准。
  • 购买记录：由 Apple 按其政策保留。

九、政策更新

我们可能适时修订本政策，并在本页更新日期；重大变更时也会在应用内提示。
